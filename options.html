Money is Time

Your income

Set your income so the extension can convert money into time. Choose the income type (hourly/weekly/monthly/yearly/daily) and enter the amount in your currency.

Display

The extension always shows the top 3 time units (e.g. "2 months 1 week 3 days" → limited to the first three non-zero units).

About

All settings are stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.sync). This extension does not transmit your income anywhere.

Tips: For yearly incomes, the extension assumes your working hours equal hours per week × 52 weeks. You can change hours per week/day above.
